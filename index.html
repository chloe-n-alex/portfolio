About Me

I am a graduate student that can be characterized by my desire to be a lifelong learner. I strive to display a willingness to learn quickly, as to better benefit myself and my institution. Flexibility and clear communication are both key to working well with others, traits which I value immensely and work to exhibit. It is my goal to contribute to the fields of higher education, history, and communication.

I seek to contribute to the world by engaging in critical thinking, promoting the values of teamwork and concise communication, supporting the arts, and constantly learning more about humanity.

I enjoy reading, writing, and traveling; you'll most likely find me in a coffee shop, library, or thrift store.
